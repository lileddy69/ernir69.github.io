How to use GymMaster Bluetooth Door Reader

Download & Login to the Member App
  1. Search for the App
  2. Click Install
  3. Get your login details
  4. Login into the app

For a more detailed description on this,

Click Here

Enable Bluetooth

Open your device’s settings app, navigate to bluetooth and enable this functionality.

Certain devices may require you to enable bluetooth Access for the GymMaster app.

For Apple devices, you need to scroll down settings until you see the GymMaster app, select this, then enable Bluetooth. Most android just take the overall device setting.

Using the App to Open Doors.
  1. Open the app
  2. Press the Bluetooth symbol at the top of the page.

If you are in range and connected successfully, the club’s doors will now be open for entry.

Range

The bluetooth readers have an effective radius of 20 meters. If you are outside this range, you will not be able to open the doors.

Open Time

By default, doors will be open for a timeframe of 3seconds from the time they receive signal. After this has ended, they will lock again.
